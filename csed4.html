Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers in the history of cricket. He is known for his aggressive batting style, leadership qualities, consistency, fitness, and passion for the game.

Former captain of the Indian Cricket Team and legendary batsman, Virat Kohli has inspired millions of cricket fans across the world.

Complete Biography

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, India. His father Prem Kohli was a criminal lawyer and his mother Saroj Kohli is a homemaker. Virat developed interest in cricket at a very young age.


He joined West Delhi Cricket Academy when he was just 9 years old. His talent and dedication quickly made him one of the best young cricketers in Delhi.


In 2008, Virat Kohli captained the Indian Under-19 team and won the ICC Under-19 World Cup. This became the turning point in his career.


Kohli made his international debut for India in August 2008. Over the years, he became one of the world's best batsmen across all formats.


Virat married famous Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in 2017. They are one of India's most loved celebrity couples.

Career Journey

2008 - International Debut

Virat Kohli made his ODI debut against Sri Lanka.

2011 - World Cup Winner

Kohli was part of the Indian team that won the ICC Cricket World Cup 2011.

2013 - ODI Ranking No.1

Became one of the top-ranked ODI batsmen in the world.

2014 - Test Captain

Became captain of the Indian Test team.

2017 - Captain in All Formats

Virat became the full-time captain of India in all formats.

2023 - World Cup Heroics

Virat Kohli broke multiple records in ICC Cricket World Cup 2023.
